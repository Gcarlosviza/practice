☞Chucky got a reboot

Ever since the second Chucky movie (child's play 2) came out, the show has been losing its fear factor. Adding humor in and out of the sequels, then getting rid of it altogether. Even though Chucky felt no longer scary, I don't think it needed to have a reboot1. I was interested to see what they would do next after bringing back Andy, who is the child from the first child's play movie but now as an adult. Hopefully, the creators of Chucky will keep their promise and continue the story but as a tv series.

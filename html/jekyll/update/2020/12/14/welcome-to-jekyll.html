R.L STINE

Dec 10, 2020

Stine was born on October 8, 1943 in Columbus, Ohio, the son of Lewis Stine, a shipping clerk, and Anne Feinstin. He grew up in Bexley,Ohio. He comes from a Jewish family. He began writing at age nine, when he found a typewriter in his attic, subsequently beginning to type stories and joke books. According to the documentary Tales from the Crypt: from Comic Bookd to Television, R.L. Stine said that he remembered reading the popular/infamous Tales from the Crypt comic books when he was young and credited as one of his inspirations. He graduated from Ohio State University in 1965 with a Bachelor of Arts In English.

He later moved to New York City to pursue his career as a writer.

He would later be know for his Goosebumps series a children's horror fiction novels. The series would be later turned in a tv show for kids, airing in 1995 to 1998.

At his peak, Stine was known to complete these stories extremely quickly, some of which were written in only six days.

Stine says he does not have any death in his stories, and the children in his novels are nver put into situations that would be considered too serious.
